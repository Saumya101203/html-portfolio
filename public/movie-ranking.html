The Best Animes according to Saumya Pratap Singh

My top 3 Animes of all time

Attack On Titan

"Attack on Titan: Humanity fights for survival behind towering walls. Giant humanoid Titans threaten their existence. Eren Yeager and his friends join the fight, uncovering dark truths about their world."

Death Note

"Death Note: A high school student gains the power to kill anyone by writing their name in a supernatural notebook. He becomes embroiled in a cat-and-mouse game with a genius detective determined to stop him."

Tokyo Ghouls

"Tokyo Ghoul: In a world where ghouls, cannibalistic creatures, live among humans, a college student becomes part ghoul after a chance encounter. He navigates the complexities of his new identity while being drawn into the conflict between ghouls and humans."
